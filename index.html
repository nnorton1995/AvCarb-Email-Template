The AvCarb Advantage

With redox flow battery developers in mind, AvCarb felts are engineered to exhibit low thruplane resistance and exceptional electrolyte flow. Our manufacturing processes ensure an ultra-high purity, highly uniform product with negligible batch-to-batch variation. All felt electrode products are precision die cut and include our proprietary Activated by AvCarb treatment. Standard thicknesses range from 2.8mm to 6.0mm and custom thicknesses are available upon request.
